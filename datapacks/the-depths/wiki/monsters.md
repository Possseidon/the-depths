## General Notes

### Attributes

- Defensive
  - **HP**: `generic.max_health`
  - **Armor**: `generic.armor`
  - **Armor Toughness**: `generic.armor_toughness`
- Offensive
  - **Damage**: `generic.attack_damage`
  - **Attack Speed**: `generic.attack_speed`
- Other
  - **Speed**: `generic.movement_speed`
  - **Knockback**: `generic.attack_knockback`
  - **Knockback Resistance**: `generic.knockback_resistance`

### Enchantments

- Defensive
  - **Protection**: `enchantment.protect.all`

### Potion Effects

- Defensive
  - **Resistance**: `potion.resistance`

### Neutral Mobs

Neutral mobs (e.g. wolfs) will target the nearest player by copying their UUID into `AngryAt`.
